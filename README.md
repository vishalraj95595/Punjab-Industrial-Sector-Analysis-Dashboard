# Punjab Corporate Landscape Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

Punjab Corporate Landscape Analysis is a Business Intelligence project developed using Power BI to explore and analyze the corporate ecosystem of Punjab, India. The project utilizes a dataset containing more than 46,000 registered companies and provides valuable insights into company registrations, industrial classifications, capital structures, and organizational status. The primary objective is to transform raw corporate data into meaningful visual insights that support data-driven decision-making and business analysis.

The dashboard offers an interactive platform for examining company distribution across various industries and sectors. It enables users to identify business trends, understand registration patterns, evaluate company categories, and analyze the relationship between authorized capital and paid-up capital. Through the use of dynamic filters, KPIs, charts, and visual reports, users can easily navigate large volumes of data and uncover important business patterns.

Data cleaning, transformation, and modeling were performed using Power Query to ensure accuracy and consistency. DAX measures were implemented to create calculated metrics and enhance analytical capabilities. The dashboard incorporates multiple visualization techniques, including bar charts, pie charts, line graphs, and summary cards, to present information in a clear and engaging format.

This project demonstrates the practical application of Business Intelligence, Data Analytics, and Data Visualization techniques in solving real-world business problems. It can be beneficial for students, researchers, analysts, policymakers, and business professionals seeking to understand Punjab's corporate landscape and economic activity. By converting complex datasets into actionable insights, the project highlights the importance of modern analytical tools in identifying trends, supporting strategic planning, and improving decision-making processes across various business domains.
